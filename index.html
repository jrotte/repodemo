Greta Van Fleet - Revitalizing Fleeting Sounds


Bio

Originating from the small town of Frankenmuth, MI, the band, Greta Van Fleet, recently rose to fame through they're uncanny Led Zeppelin like sound and powerful lyrics. Their album 'From the Fires' became the #1 digitally downloaded album on Amazon Music on November 10, 2017. This band gives hope to the revitalization of classic rock sounds in the modern era.
